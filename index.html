Generative Tree Animation

  • If it doesn't work (or while it loads) you can always watch the YouTube video. It's not so nice as the real thing because of compression artifacts.
  • Loading and generating data may take up to a minute on a Core i3 and eats around 500 MB of memory.
  • There is no loading progress.
  • One million branches are generated by default.
  • This demo requires a decent graphics card and hardware. My GPU is AMD Radeon HD 7870 and I can hear it spin up the fan when the demo is running.
  • Resolution is fixed at 1600x800.
